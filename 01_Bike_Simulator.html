Calculus Concepts While Riding 🚴📈

Derivatives (Rates of Change):

  • Velocity v(t) = dx/dt — rate of change of position
  • Acceleration a(t) = dv/dt — rate of change of velocity
  • Jerk j(t) = da/dt — rate of change of acceleration

Integrals (Accumulation):

  • Odometer x(t) = ∫v(t)dt — total distance is area under velocity curve
  • Riemann sums approximate integrals using rectangles (Left/Right/Midpoint/Trapezoid)

Calculus Tools:

  • Tangent line: Slope = derivative at that point
  • Secant → Tangent: Watch the limit definition of derivative
  • MVT point: Mean Value Theorem visualization
  • Graph switcher: View x(t), v(t), a(t), or j(t)

Simulation Settings:

  • Autopilot (A): Bike drives itself with human-like randomness — great for observing calculus!
  • Realism: Arcade (fast) or Realistic (real traffic timing)
  • Light timing: Adjust green/yellow/red durations
