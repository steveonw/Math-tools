⚠️ Common Pitfalls
Washer Method Mistake
❌ V = π∫[f(x) - g(x)]² dx
✓ V = π∫([f(x)]² - [g(x)]²) dx
Subtract areas, not radii!
Axis Offset Mistake
❌ R = f(x) when axis is y = k
✓ R = |f(x) - k|
Radius = distance to axis
Shell vs Disk Choice
💡 Use shells when integrating parallel to axis
💡 Use disks when integrating perpendicular to axis

Methods:
